Jiaming Xu 许家铭

Large Model and Agent Algorithm Researcher at Xiaomi Corporation
Multimodal LLMs · AI Agents · On-device AI · Reasoning · Memory

I am a senior algorithm expert at Xiaomi Corporation, working on large language models and AI agent systems. I am also an IEEE Senior Member and hold a senior professional title (正高级工程师).

I received my Ph.D. from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2016. After that, I worked at the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences as an assistant researcher and then associate researcher, also serving as a master's thesis advisor. I joined Xiaomi in December 2022, where my recent work spans multimodal LLMs, AI agents, on-device AI, and reasoning and memory architectures.
